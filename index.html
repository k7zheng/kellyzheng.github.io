Hi there, I'm Kelly!

I work to make autonomous systems that are beneficial to humanity and open-source tools so that more researchers can help. My background is in process controls, modelling, and simulation. I'm currently a grad student co-supervised by the fantastic Dr. Roydon Fraser and Dr. Jesse Thé and a reviewer for Elsevier Energy.

My undergrad was from the University of Waterloo's chemical engineering program with a minor in artificial intelligence. Over my degree, I interned with Hatch Controls, Automation & Electrical and Cameco Key Lake Uranium Refinery . I also had the opportunity to learn from some incredible people as part of the 2019 AI4Good cohort at MILA, the 2018 Global Engagement cohort  at the Balsillie School of International Affairs, and the 2017 Canada's Next 36 cohort.

I love learning about different domains and am happy to connect about collaboration opportunities. Drop me a line at kelly.zheng@edu.uwaterloo.ca
